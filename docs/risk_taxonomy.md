# Risk Taxonomy

- Model performance risk: weak discrimination, unstable accuracy, or inappropriate decision thresholds.
- Fairness and discrimination risk: unequal outcomes across protected or proxy groups.
- Consumer harm risk: advice or automation that worsens customer financial outcomes.
- Hallucination and misinformation risk: fabricated or unsupported statements.
- Explainability and transparency risk: insufficient reasons for model or assistant outputs.
- Robustness and drift risk: degradation under missing data, economic stress, or population change.
- Privacy and data access risk: unnecessary disclosure or misuse of customer data.
- Prompt injection and adversarial misuse risk: successful instruction override or malicious input.
- Agentic tool-use risk: unauthorized, unsafe, or poorly monitored tool calls.
- Governance and accountability risk: weak ownership, controls, monitoring, or escalation.
